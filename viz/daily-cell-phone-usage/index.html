I use my phone right before heading to bed and almost immediately after I wake up. By tracking when the screen on my phone turns on and off, I can approximate my sleep schedule. Aside from showing just how much I use my phone (scary!), this visualization also shows how irregular my sleep schedule is. By replaying my git-commit log and taking screenshots, I've made an animation of how this visualization came together.
